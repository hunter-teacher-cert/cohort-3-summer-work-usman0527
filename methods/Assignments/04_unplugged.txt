Go!
Students will have 3 minutes of independent work time to start working on the flowchart.
Students will come together as a group to determine the best way to represent the flowchart on a chartpaper (color markers and shapes will be provided).
Students will present as a group.
Observers will be given a rubric to use as they critique each flowchart.
